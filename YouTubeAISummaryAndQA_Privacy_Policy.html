Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 03/24/2025

Overview

At Karma Niti LLC ("Karma Niti," "we," "our," or "us"), we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to users of the YouTube Copilot iOS app ("YouTube Copilot"). By using YouTube Copilot, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

YouTube Copilot is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Subscription Status: Information related to your subscription plan managed via Apple's in-app purchase system
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about app usage to improve our service
  • Device Information: Standard iOS app diagnostics and crash reports

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be transparent about what we don't collect or store:

  • Your API Keys: These are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers
  • YouTube Video Transcripts: These are processed locally or sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider
  • Your Questions: Questions you ask about videos are not stored on our servers
  • Generated Summaries: AI-generated content is stored only on your device

3. How Your Information is Processed

  • Local Processing: Most data processing happens directly on your device
  • Direct API Communication: When you use the app, your device communicates directly with your chosen AI provider using your own API key - we have no access to this communication
  • No Backend Servers: We do not operate servers that process or store your transcript data, questions, or API keys

4. Subscriptions

  • Subscription Management: All subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store
  • Payment Information: We do not collect or store your payment information - all payment processing is handled by Apple
  • Subscription Status: Basic information about your subscription status is stored to enable appropriate app functionality

5. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures to safeguard any information the app does handle:

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • Direct API Communication: Communication with AI providers uses secure, encrypted connections

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Control: You can delete your local app data by uninstalling the app
  • Subscription Management: Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app. Continued use of YouTube Copilot indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
