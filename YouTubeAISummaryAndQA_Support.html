Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with YouTube Copilot?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Install the app from the App Store
  2. Go to Settings and add your API key from your preferred AI provider
  3. Paste a YouTube URL into the app
  4. Get instant summaries and start asking questions about the content

What AI providers are supported?

YouTube Copilot supports multiple AI providers:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4, GPT-3.5)
  • Azure OpenAI
  • Anthropic (Claude 3 models)
  • Google (Gemini models)

You'll need to provide your own API key from one of these providers in the app settings.

Why do I need to provide my own API key?

Using your own API key provides several benefits:

  • Enhanced privacy - your data is sent directly from your device to the AI provider
  • Full control over your AI usage and costs
  • Ability to choose your preferred AI provider
  • No intermediary servers storing your transcripts or conversations

Does YouTube Copilot work with all YouTube videos?

YouTube Copilot works with any YouTube video that has available captions or transcripts. However, it may not work with:

  • Some live streams
  • Very recent uploads where captions haven't been processed yet
  • Videos where the creator has disabled transcripts
  • YouTube Shorts (due to their format)

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your YouTube Copilot subscription directly through your Apple ID settings:

  1. Go to your iPhone/iPad Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find YouTube Copilot in your list of subscriptions
  5. From here you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription

Alternatively, you can tap the "Manage Subscription" button in the app's Subscription screen.

Is my data private and secure?

Privacy is a core principle of YouTube Copilot:

  • Your API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain and never sent to our servers
  • Transcripts and questions are processed locally or sent directly to your chosen AI provider
  • We don't store your conversations, transcripts, or summaries on our servers
  • All history is saved locally on your device

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

"API key not configured" message

To fix this issue:

  1. Tap the "Settings" button in the app
  2. Select your preferred AI provider
  3. Enter your API key from that provider
  4. Tap "Save Settings"

You can obtain API keys from:

  • OpenAI: https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
  • Google Gemini: https://ai.google.dev/
  • Claude: https://console.anthropic.com/

"Cannot access transcript" or transcript errors

If you're having trouble getting a transcript:

  1. Check that the YouTube video has captions available
  2. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  3. Try closing and reopening the app
  4. If the error persists, try another video to confirm if it's video-specific
  5. As a last resort, try using the "Reset" feature by tapping with four fingers on the screen

Subscription issues or "Monthly limit reached" messages

If you're having subscription-related issues:

  1. Check your current subscription status in the App Store
  2. If you've recently upgraded but still see limitations, restart the app
  3. Ensure your payment method is valid in your Apple ID settings
  4. If you believe there's an error with your subscription status, contact our support team

App freezing or unresponsive

If the app becomes unresponsive:

  1. Force close the app by swiping up from the bottom of your screen (or double-tapping home on older devices)
  2. Restart the app
  3. If problems persist, try restarting your device
  4. As a last resort, you can reinstall the app (your history should be preserved)
